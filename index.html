Knitting notes, personal reflections, and the small things in between — shared in hopes they might inspire someone along the way.

From Me & Binns:

Hello! I’m Nora. I picked up knitting in December 2024, and it quickly became a favorite part of my days. On Whisker Knits, I hope to share not only my projects but also tips and reflections along the way.

I also love celebrating the things that bring me happiness, and this space is for that too. One of my greatest joys is my cat, Binns, who sits with me through every stitch. Whisker Knits is dedicated to her warmth, curiosity, and the quiet moments we share.

Want to say hello? Leave me a note!
